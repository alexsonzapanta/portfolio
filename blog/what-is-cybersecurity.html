What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks.

Common Attack Types

  • Phishing
  • Malware
  • SQL Injection
  • DDoS Attacks
  • Privilege Escalation

Why Security Matters

Every device connected to the internet is a possible entry point for attackers. That’s why cybersecurity is critical in every industry — especially today.
